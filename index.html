ZOOLIGHTS

The Glow Starts With You

ZOOLIGHTS presented by Olympia Trust Company is back with more holiday cheer, festive spirit, and family fun than ever. Looking for things to do this holiday season? Come experience how 2+ million lights, 200 displays, crackling fire pits, and fun-filled activity zones (with many new additions!), transform the Calgary Zoo into a magical winter wonderland. Most magical of all, is its ability to bring friends and family together during the holiday season.  Keep an eye on this page for more details to come!

Past Events

Go Skating

Twirl the night away under a canopy of lights at the zoo’s skating rink, sponsored by Mahogany Lakeside Living. Skating becomes a musical party every Thursday, Friday and Saturday night when a DJ joins the celebrations. No skates? No worries. There are free skate rentals (courtesy of KidsSport Calgary) available on site to make sure everyone has the chance to go for a lap.

Shoot to Win

Test your Canadian skills with the Calgary Hitmen Shoot to Win challenge in zone three. Pick up a hockey stick to earn free kids vouchers and the chance to win a grand prize: box seat tickets to a Calgary Hitmen game.

Try a Virtual Pedal

ZOOLIGHTS gets active with a new Pedal Power activity, where participants can take a virtual tour of Calgary’s cycle paths by powering a screen on a stationary bike. This challenge will get visitors moving while demonstrating how much electricity is necessary for everyday activities. Education on energy conservation? Check. And it’s a fun way to challenge your friends — you’re off to the races! Come see all that zone four has to offer!

Become a Who in Zooville

The ENMAX Conservatory takes on a new theme each year, and this season it’s been transformed into Zooville. Whimsy and delight come to life in these lush gardens, where visitors young and old can dress up like Who characters (with free face painting) in the Zooville Salon. Little ones will enjoy Story Time in the butterfly gardens from Thursday to Sundays. If guests are looking for an encounter with a certain man in green, he’s creeping around every Thursday night.

Warm Up in the Tropics

The winter weather has been nipping at our noses, so take a break in the tropical environment of the ENMAX Conservatory. Zooville has transformed the garden rainforest, adding pops of colour and sparkle to the scene. The dazzling 15-ft. tree is sure to impress, and is the perfect spot for a group selfie. Stay awhile and come take a break in zone five.
